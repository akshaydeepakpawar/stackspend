# Unit Economics & Business Model

## Overview

StackSpend helps users identify unnecessary spending on AI subscriptions and discover optimization opportunities.

The product creates value by reducing software costs while generating qualified leads for consulting and optimization services.

# Economic Advantage

The audit engine is rule-based.

Benefits:

- Low inference cost
- Fast execution
- Predictable results

AI is used only for summaries, keeping operating expenses extremely low.

---

# Risks

## Pricing Changes

AI vendors frequently update pricing structures.

Mitigation:

- Maintain pricing documentation
- Review monthly
- Update recommendation rules

---

## Tool Consolidation

Users may eventually standardize on a single AI provider.

Mitigation:

Expand into:

- Spend monitoring
- Vendor benchmarking
- Usage optimization
- Procurement tooling

---

# Long-Term Opportunity

AI software spending continues to grow rapidly.

Organizations increasingly need:

- Cost visibility
- Optimization recommendations
- Procurement guidance
- Vendor management

StackSpend can evolve from a simple audit tool into a broader AI spend management platform.

---

# Conclusion

The business model benefits from:

- Extremely low operating costs
- High perceived customer value
- Multiple monetization opportunities
- Strong alignment with growing AI adoption

This creates attractive economics and a viable path toward a sustainable SaaS product.
