# Homepage Messaging Strategy

## Objective

The landing page is designed to communicate the value of StackSpend within a few seconds and encourage users to generate an AI spending audit.

The messaging focuses on:

- Cost savings
- Simplicity
- Speed
- Transparency

The goal is to help users understand what the product does before they need to scroll.

---

# Hero Section

## Headline

Stop Overpaying For AI Tools

### Why

The headline immediately communicates the primary value proposition:

- Reduce costs
- Optimize subscriptions
- Improve spending visibility

The wording is short, direct, and easy to understand.

---

## Subheadline

Analyze your AI subscriptions, discover hidden waste, and receive personalized savings recommendations in seconds.

- What outcome users receive

# FAQ

## What does StackSpend do?

StackSpend analyzes AI subscriptions and identifies opportunities to reduce spending.

---

## Which tools are supported?

Currently supported tools include ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API.

---

## How are recommendations generated?

Recommendations are generated through deterministic business rules and pricing analysis rather than AI-generated calculations.

---

## Do I need an account?

No. Audits can be generated without creating an account.

---

## Can I share reports?

Yes. Every audit generates a public shareable report URL.
