# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Overview

StackSpend helps individuals, startups, and engineering teams identify unnecessary spending across AI subscriptions and discover cost-saving opportunities.

The primary value proposition is simple:

> Save money on AI subscriptions in under 60 seconds.

The product targets organizations that have adopted multiple AI tools and lack visibility into overall spending.

---

# Target Users

## Primary Audience

### Startup Founders

Characteristics:

- Small teams
- Budget conscious
- Rapid AI adoption
- Limited procurement processes

Pain Points:

- Multiple overlapping AI subscriptions

- Difficulty evaluating ROI

---

### Engineering Teams

Characteristics:

Pain Points:

### Technical Managers

# Problem Statement

AI adoption is growing rapidly.

Many users subscribe to:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot
- Gemini

These subscriptions often overlap in functionality.

As a result:

- Costs increase
- Plans become underutilized
- Teams lose visibility into spending

Most users do not regularly audit their AI stack.

StackSpend addresses this gap.

---

Users receive:

- Spending analysis
- Cost optimization recommendations
- Savings estimates

All within minutes.

# Conversion Strategy

## Free Audit

Users can generate audits without creating an account.

Benefits:

- Lower friction
- Faster onboarding
- Higher conversion rates

## Lead Capture

After generating value, users can:
