# PROMPTS.md

## Purpose

StackSpend uses an LLM to generate a personalized audit summary based on the user's AI tool stack, spending profile, and optimization opportunities.

The audit calculations themselves are rule-based and deterministic. AI is only used to explain the results in natural language.

---

## Final Production Prompt

You are an AI cost optimization consultant.

Analyze the following audit results and generate a concise executive summary of approximately 100 words.

Requirements:
- Explain the user's current AI spending situation.
- Highlight the most important optimization opportunities.
- Mention monthly and annual savings where relevant.
- Use professional business language.
- Do not invent savings or recommendations.
- Keep the tone concise and actionable.

Input:

Current Monthly Spend: {{currentMonthlySpend}}

Monthly Savings: {{totalMonthlySavings}}

Annual Savings: {{totalAnnualSavings}}

Use Case: {{useCase}}

Recommendations:

{{recommendations}}

Generate only the summary paragraph.

---

## Why This Prompt Was Chosen

The goal is to produce a short executive-level summary that can be quickly understood by founders, engineering managers, and technical decision makers.

The prompt focuses on:

- Cost efficiency
- Actionable recommendations
- Quantified savings
- Clear business language

This avoids overly verbose AI-generated responses and keeps the report easy to share.

---

## Prompt Variations Tested

### Version 1

Generate a summary of the audit.

Issue:
Responses were generic and frequently repeated recommendation details without providing meaningful insights.

### Version 2

Act as a CFO and explain the audit.

Issue:
Responses became too formal and often exceeded the desired length.

### Version 3 (Final)

Focused on:
- Spending analysis
- Optimization opportunities
- Savings impact
- Executive summary format

This produced the most consistent results.

---

## Failure Handling

If the LLM request fails:

- The application falls back to a deterministic summary.
- Savings calculations remain accurate because they are generated by the audit engine.
- Users always receive a report even when the AI provider is unavailable.

Example fallback:

"Your current AI stack was analyzed using StackSpend's audit engine. Review the recommendations above to identify potential cost-saving opportunities and subscription optimizations."
